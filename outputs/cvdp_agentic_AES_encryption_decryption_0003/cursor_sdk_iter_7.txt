## Rationale
- **Harness/local evidence points to broken key expansion, not cipher logic:** At t=135 the debug run showed `RK0`/`rk_source`/`round_key_sched` all zero, `RK1 = 62636363626363636263636362636363` (classic `SubWord(RotWord=0)` signature), and ciphertext `f224646d...` vs expected `69c4e0d8...`. That pattern means rounds 1–10 XORed against a corrupt schedule.
- **Root cause — Icarus evaluates `sbox_enc` on stale `RotWord` in key expansion (lines 251–510):** Even with `assign RotWord` before the S-box instances, Icarus still sampled `RotWord=0` into the 40 key-schedule `sbox_enc` modules, producing the all-`0x63` RK1 seen in simulation.
- **Fix — inline `aes_sbox()` inside each step's `always_comb` (lines 39–107, 260–509):** All ten `step0`–`step9` blocks now compute `RotWord`, then call `aes_sbox(RotWord[...])` sequentially in the same procedural block. This guarantees correct evaluation order under Icarus without changing the S-box table.
- **Fix — `key_expand_src` drives expansion seed and RK0 packing (lines 22, 237–238, 513–515, 525):** `key_expand_src = (i_update_key & o_done) ? i_key : expanded_key_ff[NBW_EX_KEY-1-:NBW_KEY]` feeds `expand_in[0]` and the MSB of `expanded_key_computed`, so RK0 is `i_key` on update cycles and the stored RK0 otherwise — eliminating the zero-RK0 path seen when `valid_key` was derived separately.
- **Retained — `round_key_sched` snapshot (lines 28–29, 111–127):** On `i_start & o_done`, the full 1408-bit schedule is latched; rounds 1–10 select frozen `round_key_sched` via `rk_source_sel`, preventing mid-encryption schedule corruption.
- **Retained — `expanded_key_ff` only updates on `i_update_key & o_done` (lines 218–223):** Prevents the registered schedule from being rewritten on idle `o_done` cycles while encryption is in progress.
- **Cipher path unchanged (lines 160–216):** AddRoundKey/MixColumns XOR, GF(2⁸) multiply with conditional `0x1B` reduction, round 10 skips MixColumns, and round-key indexing via `rk_source[NBW_EX_KEY-round_ff*NBW_KEY-…]` were already correct once the schedule is fixed.
