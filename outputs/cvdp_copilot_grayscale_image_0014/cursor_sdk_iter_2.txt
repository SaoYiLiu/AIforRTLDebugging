## Rationale
- **Row 2 summation omits one term (line 66):** `pipeline_sum_stage11` is computed as `mult_result3 + mult_result4`, leaving out `mult_result5`. Row 2 of a 3×3 kernel must include all three products.
- **Harness evidence:** At 120 ns, cocotb reports `Error in Row 2 summation!` with `pipeline_sum_stage11` = 2 vs expected 3 (`assert 2 == 3`).
- **Matches test stimulus:** With unit inputs/kernels, each `mult_result` is 1; row 2 should be `1+1+1=3`, but the buggy sum is `1+1=2`.
- **Rows 1 and 3 are correct (lines 65, 67):** `pipeline_sum_stage10` and `pipeline_sum_stage12` already sum three terms each; only row 2 is wrong.
- **Normalization divisor is wrong (line 85):** Division by 8 is incorrect for a 3×3 kernel; the test explicitly expects division by 9 (`"Error in normalization: Division should be by 9!"`).
- **Minimal fix:** Add `mult_result5` to the row-2 sum and change `/ 8` to `/ 9`.
